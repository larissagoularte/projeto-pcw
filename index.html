PetBuddy is a home interaction robot designed to provide companionship, entertainment, and care for pets. It can perform a variety of functions to ensure pets are always happy and healthy, even when no one is home. With advanced features, PetBuddy can play with your pets, monitor their well-being, and offer comfort, making it an essential companion for pet owners who want the best for their furry friends.

Main Features

AI System for Pet Habits

Analyzes and learns animal habits, predicting needs and offering proactive care.

Automated Feeding System

Equipped with a food dispenser and retractable bowl, feeds pets based on AI predictions or owner commands, reducing waste and mess.

Water Storage System

Ideal for long absences, with automatic refill via sensors or remote command.

Health Monitoring

Tracks physical metrics and activities, generating detailed health reports.

Video and Audio

Includes camera, microphone, and LCD screen for real-time video and audio communication with pets.

Omnidirectional Wheels

Moves freely around the house, following the pet using proximity sensors.

Mechanical Arms for Playing

Interactive arms to hold and move toys, like cat wands and dog balls.

Mobile App Control

Full control via mobile app with notifications.
